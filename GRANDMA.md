# GRANDMA.md

## What this project is about

You have a long car brochure and want to find specific information quickly without reading the whole thing.

You can upload that brochure and ask questions like:
- "What kind of engine does the car have?"
- "What features are there?"
- "What kind of person is this car made for?"

The system reads the document for you and gives you a clear answer.

---

## How it works (a simple explanation)

1. You send in a PDF brochure.
2. The system breaks it up into smaller parts
3. It finds the most relevant parts when you ask a question.
4. It puts those pieces together to make an answer.

It only answers based on what is actually in the document.

---

## Why it is helpful

- Cuts down on the time it takes to read long documents 
- Helps you find information quickly   
- Sticking to the original source cuts down on mistakes   

---

## What it can't do

- It can't answer if the brochure doesn't have the information  
- It doesn't "know everything"
- If it can't find the right part of the document, it might miss something.

---

## In one sentence

It is like a smart assistant that reads a document for you and only uses what is written in it to answer your questions.
