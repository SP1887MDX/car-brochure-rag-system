# GRANDMA.md 

## What this project is for

Think about having a car brochure that is long and hard to read.

You can upload that brochure and then ask questions like this:

* "What kind of engine does the car have?"
* "What features are there?"
* "Who is this car for?"

The system finds the m# GRANDMA.mdost important parts of the document and gives you a clear answer instead of making you read everything yourself.

--- 

## How it works (in simple terms)

1. You upload a PDF, like a brochure for a car.
2. The system cuts it up into small pieces
3. It looks for the most relevant pieces when you ask a question.
4. It uses those parts to come up with an answer.
